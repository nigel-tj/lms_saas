# Loan Officer Help

This guide is for **Loan Officers**. You do most of your work in the borrower portal. This page tells you how to sign in, where to find things, and what to do each day.

> Last updated: 22 July 2026.

## What you do here

- Meet new borrowers and help them fill in their first forms.
- Check their identity documents and consent.
- Take a new loan application from “draft” to “ready for the manager.”
- See the borrowers assigned to you and what stage each one is at.

## Sign in

1. Open the sign-in page in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge. Your administrator will give you the link.
2. Type your staff email and password.
3. You will land on the **Officer Portal**, which is your home page.

If you land on the borrower page instead, or you get a message that you do not have permission, your role is not set up correctly. Ask a Branch Manager or your administrator to fix your profile.

## Your home page

The Officer Portal shows your daily work in one place:

- The **Pipeline** lists borrowers you are helping and what step they are at.
- The **Quick actions** buttons take you to the most common tasks.
- The **Reports** link shows the part of the loan book assigned to you.

## A normal day

1. Open the Officer Portal. Look at the pipeline.
2. For each borrower, work the next step:
   - **New borrower** — start the onboarding form to create their account, link the right branch, and capture their national ID.
   - **KYC** — open their compliance record, mark identity documents as seen, and record their consent.
   - **Application** — finish the loan application, double-check the amount, term, and branch, then submit it for your manager.
   - **Disbursement** — the back-office team handles the actual payout once the manager approves.
3. Use the **Support** link in the sidebar to report any problem with the portal itself.

## Onboarding a new borrower

The **Onboard User** form is the only way to create a new borrower. Only Loan Officers can open it. When you fill in the form:

- The system creates the borrower’s user account, their contact record, and their customer record for you.
- Their national ID is captured automatically and is ready for the KYC step.
- You do not need to set anything else up by hand.

## Checking identity (KYC)

Open the borrower’s **Compliance** record. Fill in:

- KYC status (Approved, Pending, or Rejected).
- Whether they have given consent for the pilot, and the date.
- A copy of their ID and proof of address.

Submit the record. The system will not let a loan application go through unless KYC is Approved and consent is recorded.

## When something is stuck

| What you see | What it means | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| “You may only create Borrower accounts” | Your role is Loan Officer, not Manager or Admin | Ask a Manager or Admin to do it |
| You cannot see loans for your branch | Your branch permission is not set | Ask your administrator to grant it |
| The application will not submit | Identity check is not Approved, or a limit has been reached | Look at the borrower’s compliance record, and check with the manager about limits |

## When you have learned the basics

You can tick these off:

- [ ] Sign in and reach the Officer Portal.
- [ ] See the borrowers assigned to you.
- [ ] Open a loan application and submit it through the identity and consent checks.
- [ ] Create a new borrower using the onboarding form without an error.

## Need help?

For problems with the portal, open a **Support** ticket from the sidebar. For anything else, ask a Branch Manager.
