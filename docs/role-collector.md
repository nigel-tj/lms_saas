# Collector Help

This guide is for **Collectors**. You do most of your work in the field, often with one hand and a weak phone signal. The Collection Run page is built for that: it works offline, and it tells you what is saved and what is still waiting to upload.

> Last updated: 22 July 2026.

## What you do here

- See the day’s collection list for your branch.
- Record payments in the field, online or offline.
- Sync your work back to the office when you have signal.

## Sign in

1. Open the sign-in page in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge. Your administrator will give you the link.
2. Type your staff email and password.
3. You will land on the **Collection Run** page.

If you land on the borrower home page instead, your profile is not set up as a Collector. Ask a Branch Manager or your administrator to fix it.

## The run sheet

The run sheet is the first thing you see. It shows every borrower you should visit today, in the order your branch prefers.

- A green **Synced** badge next to a row means the office has already received the latest update for that borrower.
- A round **Pending** badge means the office has not seen the latest update yet. Tap the row to retry, or wait — the page will keep trying.

You can filter the run sheet by **Branch** and by **Officer** so you only see your own list.

## Recording a repayment

1. In the run sheet, tap the row for the borrower.
2. Choose **Record repayment**.
3. Type the amount, the date you received it, and any notes.
4. If you have signal, tap **Submit**. The row turns green within a few seconds.
5. If you do not have signal, tap **Save offline**. The row stays round until you are back online, and then it will turn green on its own.

If the office says the payment was recorded twice, do not record it again. A Loan Officer will cancel the duplicate.

## Working without signal

- The page works without a network. Anything you save is kept on your device.
- When signal returns, the page syncs automatically. The **Synced** / **Pending** badge on each row tells you the status.
- If the **Loading run sheet…** message never goes away, pull down to refresh the page. If it still does not work, ask the office to refresh your view from their side.

## When something is stuck

| What you see | What it means | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| “Loading run sheet…” never finishes | The page could not download today’s list | Pull down to refresh. If it still fails, call the office. |
| A row is stuck on **Pending** | The office has not received your update | Tap the row and choose **Retry sync**. |
| You cannot find a borrower on the list | You may not have permission for that branch | Ask a Branch Manager |
| You recorded the same payment twice | The office needs to cancel one | Stop and call the office |

## When you have learned the basics

You can tick these off:

- [ ] Sign in and reach the Collection Run page.
- [ ] Filter the run sheet to your branch and your name.
- [ ] Record a repayment while online and see the row turn green.
- [ ] Save a repayment while offline, see the row stay round, and watch it turn green when you get signal back.

## Need help?

| Problem | Who to ask |
|---|---|
| You cannot find a borrower | Branch Manager (they may need to widen your branch permission) |
| A payment was recorded twice | Loan Officer (they will cancel the duplicate) |
| Sync is stuck | Log out and back in. If it still fails, ask the office. |
