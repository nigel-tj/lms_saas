# Borrower Help

This guide is for people who borrow from us. It explains how to sign in, see your loans, apply for a new loan, make payments, download statements, and get help.

> Last updated: 22 July 2026.

## What you can do here

- See all the loans in your name in one place.
- See how much you still owe and when your next payment is due.
- Apply for a new loan when your branch asks you to.
- Pay online if your branch has online payments turned on.
- Download a PDF statement of any loan.
- Open a support ticket if something is wrong.

## Sign in

1. Open the sign-in page in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge. Your branch will give you the link (it usually looks like `https://your-company.kesari.africa/login`).
2. Type the email and password your loan officer created for you.
3. You will land on your **Portfolio Dashboard**, which is the home page for borrowers.

If you forgot your password, click **Forgot password?** on the sign-in page. If your email does not match the one on your account, you will see an empty dashboard — call your branch to fix the email.

## Your home page

The home page is the first screen you see after signing in. It shows:

| Card | What it tells you |
|---|---|
| Outstanding | The total amount you still owe across all your loans |
| Active loans | How many loans you have right now |
| Next due | Your next payment and the date it is due |
| Risk mix | How your loans are split between healthy, watchlist, and overdue |

To open a loan, click the loan card. You will see the schedule, what you have paid so far, and what is still left.

## Apply for a new loan

Use this only when your branch has asked you to apply online.

1. In the sidebar on the left, click **Apply**.
2. Fill in the loan amount, how long you want to take it over, and the reason for the loan.
3. Attach the documents your officer asked for (ID, payslip, etc.).
4. Click **Submit for review**. Your officer will pick it up from there.

## Make a payment

Online payments are only available if your branch has turned them on. You will see a **Make Payment** link in the sidebar if so.

1. In the sidebar, click **Make Payment**.
2. Pick the loan you are paying toward.
3. Type the amount.
4. Pick a way to pay (EcoCash, OneMoney, or bank transfer).
5. Click **Submit**. You will see a confirmation on the screen. If your branch has SMS or email turned on, you will also get a message.

## Download a statement

1. Open the loan you want a statement for.
2. Click **Download Statement**. A PDF will save to your computer.

If the button is missing, your branch has not set up statement printing yet. Call them to ask.

## If something looks wrong

| What you see | What it usually means | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| Empty home page after signing in | Your sign-in email does not match the one on your account | Call your branch |
| A “Loading…” message that never goes away on Apply or Pay | The system had a problem | Wait a minute, then refresh. If it still does not work, note the time and tell your branch. |
| A loan is missing that you expect | The loan has not been finalised in the office yet | Ask your officer to confirm |
| Statement PDF will not download | Statement printing is not set up | Call your branch |

## Opening a support ticket

In the sidebar, click **Support**. A short form opens where you describe the problem.

A few important things to know:

- Any file you attach is stored privately. Only staff who are allowed to see your case can open it.
- The download link is single-use and expires. Do not share it with anyone outside the branch.
- The system hides sensitive numbers (like your national ID and account number) in lists and logs. If you see a number that looks wrong, do not share it — call your branch.

## When you have learned the basics

You can tick these off:

- [ ] Sign in and see your loans on the home page.
- [ ] Open a loan and read the schedule and history.
- [ ] Download a statement PDF (or know to call the branch if the button is missing).
- [ ] Open a support ticket and attach a file without sharing a public link.

## Need to reach us?

For account or loan questions, call or visit your branch. For a problem with the website itself, open a **Support** ticket from the sidebar.
